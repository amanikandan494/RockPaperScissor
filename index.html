Let's play Frog,Slug and Snake Game

Description:This game is inspired by the true origin of rock,paper and scissors: "sansukumi-ken".
The game has three entities:the frog,the slug and the snake, who are afraid of one another.
Frog wins against the slug, which in turn wins against the snake and the snake wins against the frog.
You are pitted against the computer in this game, you should choose one option amongst the three given below and
the computer will also choose one option in each round. If you win against computer, you get one point in one round
if computer is the winner it gets one point in one round. The first person to reach 5 score points wins the game
